Announcing our 2023 冬天 大逃杀 Kickoff

Collegiate Crash 团队 | 2023 年 11 月 6 日

Disclaimer: This tournament is not affiliated with or sponsored by Electronic Arts Inc.

We are excited to share this news with all of you, about our plans for hosting Collegiate Crash: 2023 Winter Battle Royale Kickoff; coming to you this coming winter (as the name suggests). For this tournament, we have made to decision to be featuring Apex Legends as the main game title in which our participants will be competing in for a prize pool of an equivalent to ≈100 USD. As this tournament is one of our first, we have also waived off our signup fee for this tournament giving essentially free entry for our participants. For more information about this tournament, kindly click here and or to get started with the sign-up process, get more frequent updates, click here to join our Discord, which is our primary point of operations. We would highly recommend joining our scrims, possibly for your own and your teams growth and maybe connect with other or know your competition. More info on joining our scrims.
