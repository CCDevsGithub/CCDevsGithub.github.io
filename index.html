Who are we?

We at Collegiate Crash are trying to provide a platform to elevate collegiate athletics on a global scale in a step by step manner. With our premiere selection of sports and e-sports given current relevance, popularity and a host of other variables is where we primarily intend to focus our resources to create competitive infrastructures. As methods to develop these infrastructures of our respective selection of sports and e-sports, a few ways we do so is through hosting events such as but not limited to tournaments, scrims, leagues etc. We do have plans to introduce additional sports and e-sports as we progress.
