Disclaimer: This Tournament is not affiliated with or sponsored by Electronic Arts Inc.
The HWUMSA Sports Invitational Apex Legends Tournament 2024 is organized by HWUMSA (Heriot-Watt University Malaysia Student Association). The tournament is invitational and exclusive to universities in Malaysia (university registration fee is RM 30 per team upon invitation).Target universities to be invited are as follows: Heriot-Watt, MMU, Cyberjaya, Nottingham, Southampton, Taylors, Monash, INTI, APU and Sunway. The tournament spans over four days from March 7th - 10th (online). The tournament is divided into two splits.
